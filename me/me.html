The belief in mermaids may have arisen at the very dawn of our species. Magical female figures first appear in cave paintings in the late Paleolithic (Stone Age) period some 30,000 years ago, when modern humans gained dominion over the land and, presumably, began to sail the seas. Gezellig (pronounced huh-ZEL-uhk or guh-ZELL-uhk) is a cornerstone Dutch word describing a warm, cozy, and social atmosphere, often translated as "cozy" "convivial" or "togetherness". It encompasses pleasant, relaxed social situations, such as having a drink with friends, a charming cafe, or a warm, inviting home.
